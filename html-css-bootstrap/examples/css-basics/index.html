Marriott School of Business

The Marriott School of Business is part of Brigham Young University in Provo, UT. It has the following programs:

  • Information Systems
  • Accounting
  • Finance
  • Management
  • Recreation Management

In 2013, the Accounting program was ranked 1st in the nation. The Marriott School often places very high in a variety of categories. You can learn on the official Marriott School website.

The school mission

Education

Attract, develop, and place men and women of faith, character, and professional ability who will become outstanding leaders capable of dealing with change in a competitive global environment.

Scholarship

Advance knowledge using strong conceptual and spiritual foundations to identify and solve management problems.

Service

Serve in academic, professional, and civic organizations, extending the benefits of management education and scholarship to the university, to members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and to the world.
